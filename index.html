How it Work's ?

Follow the steps below to add a zipcode widgets to your store.

  • * Activate the widget: Go to "Settings" and enable the widget (default is disabled).
  • * Import Zipcodes: Go to Import section and upload a CSV File containing all zipcodes.
  • * Customize the widget settings: Customize with many customization options to design the zipcode widget as per your choice.
  • * Test before making live: In the "Appearance Section", you can verify the whole settings.
